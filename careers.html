Careers

We are more intent on moulding well-rounded businessmen for the future, rather than developing individuals as part of an efficient assembly line.

Why Altus?

Here at Altus, we offer the opportunity for every employee to grow as an individual in an environment that is open and transparent.
  • We provide an environment that is transparent, open and without politics
  • Working in small teams, our employees put all their energy into the work
  • We train everyone from scratch
  • Altus’ corporate financiers will have developed a strong sense of commercial awareness, setting them in good stead with the necessary skills to either excel in the industry or to pursue their own business goals in different fields.

What we offer?

Our work is about far more than just numbers, and our reach stretches across Greater China and Japan. With the diverse range of projects and tasks you’ll be involved in, you’ll find your role here is both fulfilling and challenging.
Diversity and breadth of work
An insight into every aspect of our business and first-hand exposure
Broad Business Expertise
Allowing you to build up your own network and experience
Project ownership
A sense of ownership of every project you handle

Who we look for?

We are always looking for exceptional individuals with the drive, commitment and confidence to succeed.
Ability to combine intellectual rigor with common sense and insight
The generalist nature of the corporate financier role means that strong candidates from all academic backgrounds are welcome; indeed, the skills of arts and humanities graduates are as integral to our work as those with more quantitative backgrounds.
Communication and confidence
Corporate financiers are expected to be as comfortable with words as they are with numbers. Our line of work involves a lot of client-facing activities, so confidence and excellent verbal and written communication skills are a must in order to thrive in this fast-paced industry.
Desire to succeed and excel
We are particularly interested in recent graduates with a desire to succeed and excel. Our team is international in both nature and outlook. We encourage talent worldwide to apply. We will handle all travel and visa arrangements for successful overseas candidates.
