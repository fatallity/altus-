Altus group specialises in corporate finance and property investment in Japan and Hong Kong

Altus Holdings Limited is a company listed on the Growth Enterprise Market of The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited (stock code: 8149). Our group focuses on corporate finance and property investment. We have been building up our expertise in both areas since 2001, when we successfully obtained the necessary licences for corporate finance work in Hong Kong and invested in our first real estate interest in Japan.

We specialise in providing corporate finance and advisory, real estate investment management services, and investing in a portfolio of investment properties in Japan. [Altus Capital Limited is licensed by the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission and an approved sponsor for IPOs on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. ] Altus Holdings Limited is a company listed on the Growth Enterprise Market of The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited (Stock code: 8149).

Corporate Finance

We primarily offer sponsorship, financial advisory and compliance advisory services to our clients through our subsidiaries Altus Capital Limited and Altus Investments Limited. Altus Capital Limited is licensed by the Hong Kong Securities and Future Commission (“SFC”) and an approved sponsor for IPOs on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. Altus Investments Limited is licensed by the SFC for dealing in securities.

Property investment

We specialise in real estate investment in Japan and Hong Kong. Over the years, in addition to our own investments in Japan, we also garnered experience in the Japan property market through participating in investment management of Japan residential real estate.
